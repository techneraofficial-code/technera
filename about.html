About Us

We're a Small Team That Ships Big Things

TechNera is a founder-led digital product company based in London. We build custom websites, educational platforms, and AI automation systems for ambitious teams across the UK.

Our Story

TechNera was born from a frustration every founder knows: the gap between what agencies promise and what they deliver. Too many businesses end up with bloated timelines, inflated budgets, and products that miss the mark.

We started TechNera to do things differently. Every project is led by the people who actually build the product — engineers and designers who care about craft, not just invoices. We keep our team lean so we can stay nimble, and we treat every client's business as if it were our own.

Our work speaks for itself. From GCSEMaths helping thousands of UK students smash their exams, to SATACTMath boosting SAT scores for students worldwide, to IngredScan making grocery shopping safer with AI — we build products that people genuinely love and rely on every day.

Our Values

What We Believe In

01

Craft Over Speed

We ship fast, but never at the expense of quality. Every line of code is written with intention, and every interface is designed with the end user in mind.

02

Radical Transparency

No hidden fees, no scope surprises, no black boxes. You'll always know what we're building, why, and how much it costs. Weekly demos, open communication.

03

Outcomes, Not Outputs

We don't measure success by features shipped — we measure it by business results. More users, higher conversion, less manual work. That's what matters.

04

Long-Term Thinking

We build products that scale. Clean architecture, documented code, and maintainable systems — because we want your product to thrive long after our engagement ends.
